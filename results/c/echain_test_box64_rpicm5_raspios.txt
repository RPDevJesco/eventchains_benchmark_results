EventChains v3.1.0 - Security-Hardened Build (No Magic Numbers)
Features:
  - Reference counting for memory safety
  - Constant-time comparisons for sensitive data
  - Memory usage limits (10 MB max)
  - Iterative middleware execution (max 16 layers)
  - Reentrancy protection
  - Signal safety
  - Function pointer validation
  - Configurable error detail levels
  - Overflow protection on all arithmetic
  - Secure memory zeroing
  - Optimized: No magic number overhead

Benchmark Configuration:
  Iterations per test: 10000
  Timing precision: nanoseconds
  Work per event: ~100 operations + string formatting

|---------------------------------------------------------------|
|  TIER 1: Minimal Baseline (Cost of Orchestration Framework)  |
|---------------------------------------------------------------|

Baseline: 3 bare function calls
EventChains: Full pattern with 0 middleware
Iterations: 10000

Results:
----------------------------------------------------------------
Baseline (3 function calls)        : avg=  3.111 us  min=  3.074 us  max= 37.629 us  stddev= 0.387 us
EventChains (0 middleware)         : avg=  3.560 us  min=  2.222 us  max=  9.833 us  stddev= 0.254 us

EventChains Overhead               :  +14.43% ( +0.449 us overhead)

----------------------------------------------------------------
0 middleware layers                : avg=  2.247 us  min=  2.204 us  max=  9.722 us  stddev= 0.089 us
1 middleware layer                 : avg=  2.755 us  min=  2.722 us  max=  5.278 us  stddev= 0.046 us
3 middleware layers                : avg=  2.816 us  min=  2.778 us  max=  5.870 us  stddev= 0.062 us
5 middleware layers                : avg=  2.849 us  min=  2.814 us  max=  6.407 us  stddev= 0.059 us
10 middleware layers               : avg=  2.945 us  min=  2.925 us  max=  5.241 us  stddev= 0.054 us

Overhead Analysis:
----------------------------------------------------------------
0 -> 1 middleware:  +0.508 us total, 0.508 us per layer
1 -> 3 middleware:  +0.061 us total, 0.030 us per layer
3 -> 5 middleware:  +0.033 us total, 0.017 us per layer
5 -> 10 middleware:  +0.096 us total, 0.019 us per layer

Amortized cost per middleware layer: 0.070 us
